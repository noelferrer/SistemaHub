## Our Process

We work closely with every client. Here's how we move from problem to product:

1. **Understand** — We run a deep discovery session to map the real problem, the existing workflow, and the people who will use the software. We don't open a code editor until we understand the goal.

2. **Design** — We build the solution in structure first — user flows, wireframes, and UX architecture. You see the full picture before we build anything.

3. **Build** — Development is iterative and transparent. You see progress at every stage, provide feedback, and stay in the loop. No black-box development.

4. **Ship** — We handle deployment, QA, and launch. The software is tested, documented, and handed over to you — running and ready.

5. **Support** — After launch, we stay close. Whether it's maintenance, improvements, or new features, we're a long-term partner — not a one-time vendor.
